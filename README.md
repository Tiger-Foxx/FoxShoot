## ✨ About

**FoxShoot** is a powerful desktop application that uses advanced neural network algorithms to upscale and enhance your images and videos. Our engine is built on a highly optimized version of a well-known upscaling algorithm, fine-tuned specifically for:

- ⚡ **Maximum speed** on consumer hardware
- 🎮 **GPU acceleration** via Vulkan (works on NVIDIA, AMD, and Intel)
- 💪 **Low-resource operation** - works smoothly even on modest systems
- 🎨 **Anime & photo optimization** - separate processing paths for best results

Transform your 720p videos to 4K, or upscale your photos up to 4x their original resolution with stunning quality.

---

## 🚀 Features

### Core Capabilities

- **Image Upscaling** - Support for PNG, JPG, WEBP, BMP formats
- **Video Upscaling** - Support for MP4, MKV, AVI, WebM, MOV formats
- **Scale Factors** - 2x, 3x, or 4x upscaling
- **Batch Processing** - Process multiple files at once

### User Experience

- 🌙 **Beautiful Dark UI** - Modern, eye-friendly interface
- 🌍 **Multilingual** - English & French support
- 📁 **Drag & Drop** - Simply drop files to start
- 📊 **Real-time Progress** - See exactly what's happening
- 🖼️ **Before/After Comparison** - Slider view to compare results
- 🔧 **Custom Output Folder** - Choose where to save results

### Windows Integration

- 🖱️ **Right-Click Menu** - "Enhance with FoxShoot" on any image/video
- 🔔 **System Tray** - Runs quietly in background
- 💾 **Queue Persistence** - Resume your work after restart

## 🎯 Usage

### Quick Start

1. **Launch FoxShoot** from desktop or Start Menu
2. **Drag & Drop** your images or videos onto the app
3. **Select scale factor** (2x, 3x, or 4x)
4. **Click Start** and wait for the magic ✨
5. **Find your files** in the output folder

### Right-Click Integration

After installation, right-click any image or video and select **"🦊 Enhance with FoxShoot"** to start processing immediately!

### Video Options

- **Quality**: Low (fast), Medium (balanced), High (best)
- **Format**: MP4, MKV, or WebM output
- **Audio**: Keep or remove audio track

## 💻 Requirements

### Minimum

- Windows 10 (64-bit)
- 4 GB RAM
- Any GPU with Vulkan support _or_ CPU-only mode

### Recommended

- Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
- 8 GB RAM
- NVIDIA GTX 1060 / AMD RX 580 / Intel UHD 630 or better
- SSD for faster processing

### Supported GPUs

- **NVIDIA**: GTX 900 series and newer
- **AMD**: RX 400 series and newer
- **Intel**: UHD 600 series and newer
